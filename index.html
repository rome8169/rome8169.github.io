I am an assistant professor at the School of Mathematical Sciences, East China Normal University. I am interested in de Rham theory, Hodge theory, periods and fundamental groups of algebraic varieties, particularly those of curves and their moduli spaces. My work is described here in an elementary way (undergraduate level), and in a slide for the 8th International Congress of Chinese Mathematicians.
